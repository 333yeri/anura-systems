## STYLE & TECHNICAL NOTES (Apply to All Prompts)

**Visual Quality Reference:** GTA IV era (2008) — ~30,000 polygons per character model, not hyper-modern PBR, not photorealistic, but modern enough to feel immersive. Think: detailed low-poly with good textures and lighting, not CS:Source or PS1.

**Color Palette:**
- Void black: #0D0F12
- Mist dark: #1A1D24
- Neon cyan: #00F3FF
- Ember amber: #D4AF37
- Forest greens: deep emerald to dark pine

**Mood Arc Across Acts:**
- Act 1: Isolation, digital, anticipation (cold, black, phosphor green)
- Act 2: Surrender, transition, weightlessness (indigo sky → green canopy)
- Act 3: Mystery, guided journey, alive-but-creepy (dark green, bioluminescence, amber beacon)
- Act 4: Sanctuary, warmth, earned arrival (amber fire, cool forest, invitation)

**Camera:** First-person POV throughout Acts 2-4. Mouse-controlled smooth lerp. Act 1 is third-person looking at the CRT. The human user is never visible in the world; the camera is the only viewpoint.

**Key Characters:**
- The Frog: small, green-brown, low-poly, calm personality, acts as guide
- No humans, bodies, hands, or human silhouettes appear inside the interactive world
